About Athlete Performance Predictor

The Athlete Performance Predictor is designed to help High School athletes and College coaches to predict and plan for the transition to collegiate track. The Athlete Performance Predictor uses your High School PR from Junior Year, and compares it to historical data in order to predict your collegiate Freshman year performance.

Developed by a Track and Field athlete and Data Science Student, my goal is to provide a resource for college recruiting. These models are not perfect and are only meant to be used as some insight into athlete's progression and potential.

Features

  • Performance predictions for various track and field events
  • Data-driven insights from historical athlete performances
  • User-friendly interface for easy access to predictions

More information can be found on this project on: https://github.com/sambowerman1/athlete-predictor
